WHAT IS RAINBOW SIX SIEGE ?

Rainbow Six: Siege is a tactical shooter where the player takes on the role of various agents from several special forces units and task forces that make up the Rainbow Team. Like the other titles in the series, it focuses heavily on team play and the "realism" of operations.

However, there are major differences from earlier versions of the game, with a strong multiplayer focus and fully destructible environments. A total of 10 maps, the American SWAT, the British SAS, the German GSG 9, the Russian Spetsnaz and the French GIGN are available at the time of release. Currently, 24 intervention units and 21 maps are available in Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Siege.

For the first time in a Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six game, players conduct a new style of attack where enemies can turn their environment into modern fortresses to keep the advantage over Rainbow Six teams that assault to break their position. The game system encourages players to fortify their position, by reinforcing walls, placing barbed wire to slow down their enemies, regularly watching the cameras, deploying mines and shields, or, to break through enemy defences, using observation drones or rappelling.

All operators (defenders or attackers) have their own unique skills (represented by a specific weapon or gadget) and abilities (speed and protection). They also have weapons related to their unit of intervention, or more specific to the agent.

AGENTS & MAPS

    • Osa

      The transparent, armored Talon-8 shield can be carried by Osa or deployed on the ground and window frames, giving her a protected line of sight as she strategizes her attack.

    • Thunderbird

      When deployed on the ground, the Kóna Station becomes a safe place for injured or downed agents. All they have to do is approach the gadget, and the Kóna Station will naturally increase the agent's HP or revive him

    • Flores

      Controlled remotely, the RCE-Ratero charge moves forward as Flores searches for a location to detonate it. Once detonated, the RCE-Ratero charge clings to the nearest surface, covers itself with an armored shell and explodes, destroying nearby destructible gadgets and walls.

    • Aruni

      Once in place, the portal projects a laser beam capable of detecting the slightest movement through it. When an intruder is detected, the energy emitted by the laser increases drastically, causing second and third degree burns.

    • zero

      His Argus launcher is equipped with a special type of camera that can pierce destructible and reinforced surfaces to monitor both sides. Another feature of the Argus cameras is a single-use laser beam, perfect for destroying key elements of the enemy's defenses or for drawing attention away from bottlenecks.

    • Ace

      His gadget uses hydraulic pressure to break down reinforced and destructible surfaces. The cylinder sticks to where Ace sends it and it deploys. It causes one or two explosions depending on where it is stuck. Each of its explosions leaves a gaping hole in the wall for an agent to get through.

    • Melusi

      The Banshee gadget can be deployed on any surface. As soon as an enemy enters its range, it emits a sound and slows down its progress. It can be destroyed, of course, but since it's bulletproof, attackers will have to use utilities or get close to it to hit it.

    • Oryx

      The Remah Sprint allows him to patrol efficiently and cover short distances with unparalleled speed. It can also be used to stun opponents and sprint through destructible walls, creating an opening. Be careful though, as he loses health every time Sprint Remah is used to destroy a wall.

    • Iana

      The Gemini Replicator is a holographic copy of Iana, controlled from a distance. It can move and make noises as it would, but the copy cannot shoot, attack in close combat or use gadgets. Its duration is limited and it can be deactivated at any time. There is always a recharge time between uses, which is longer if the copy is destroyed.

    • Wamai

      The Mag-NET system is a throwable adhesive gadget. It sticks to surfaces and waits. Its function is to attract an enemy's projectile to its position and then self-destruct by detonating the projectile.

    • Kali

      To talk about the LV explosive spear, we have to mention the CSRX 300 rifle from Kali. It is a dangerous weapon that can pierce barricades and hatches in one shot, and create large holes in destructible walls. The LV is mounted under the barrel and can be used in conjunction with the CSRX 300's scope, and its purpose is to destroy gadgets with a light blast within a defined perimeter. When used on a destructible wall, it creates a hole slightly smaller than a shot from the rifle. When fired at a reinforced wall, however, it simply lodges on its surface.

    • Amaru

      The Garra hook is an absolutely exceptional device. Thanks to it, Amaru is able to cling to ledges and windows to get up in record time. From now on, not only his opponents have to be careful of attacks coming from above, but also of those coming from below. Thanks to the Garra, Amaru is the only agent who can climb up through an open hatch as well as down. With the right timing, it is possible to perform his famous Garra Kick by pulling the hook on a window: this directly eliminates an opponent located on the other side of the window.

    • Goyo

      Like ordinary deployable shields, the Volcan shield provides cover and allows defenders to block the way, but Goyo spices things up with a firebomb attached to it. From the front, though, it's impossible to tell what kind of shield it is. Until someone shoots the bomb to detonate it, the shield blocks the attackers.

    • Nokk

      The HEL is a glove that reduces ambient sound output on activation and hides Nøkk's image from enemy observation tools. Thus, she will be completely undetectable by drones, armored cameras, Evil Eyes and the like, and she will be able to patrol without being seen. In addition, its footsteps will not make any noise, allowing it to silently track down opposing agents.

    • Warden

      The function that the Gaze performs was almost non-existent among defenders. While active, Warden can see through smoke and reverse the lack of intelligence inflicted on his team. By activating his Smart Glasses, Warden can also protect himself from blinding effects, or regain his sight during a blind: this allows him to regain control of those few seconds of danger. However, the heat vision of the Gaze only works if Warden stays still. If he moves, he will be affected by the smoke, like other agents.
